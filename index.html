Questions? We’ve answers.

Where can I download the Smartlearn application from?

To get the Smartlearn app, simply visit the App Store for iOS users or the Play Store for Android users.

What is the school ID and where do I get that from?

A Student ID is a distinct identification number assigned to every student, serving as the reference for their school records. This ID is provided to both the student and parent upon admission. If you haven’t received it or are uncertain about your school ID, please reach out to your child’s class teacher or the admissions counsellor for assistance.

Can I edit my name, address, etc. in my profile?

The majority of the information in your Smartlearn platform profile is automatically updated based on the details you provided in the admission form. However, you have the option to edit social media links, achievements and your profile picture. If any of the system-entered information is incorrect or requires an update, you can submit a ticket directly through the application for the necessary changes.

How many modules and lessons are offered on the Smartlearn platform?

Currently, there are 150 modules available on the Smartlearn platform and this library of modules will expand further as Smartlearn continues to evolve as a dynamic AI-driven platform, constantly adding new content and features to enhance the learning experience.

What kind of interactive elements does this platform offer?

As an AI-powered platform, Smartlearn provides a wide array of interactive elements both during and after lessons, including games and quizzes featuring audio-visual questions. These engaging features enhance the learning experience and make it more dynamic and enjoyable for students.
